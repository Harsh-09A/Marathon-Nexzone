Welcome to Marathon Nexzone

A tranquil life by the mountains, exciting adventures amid vast green pastures, and the gentle sound of leaves billowing in the breeze - this is a picturesque everyday life at Marathon Nexzone. A hidden gem in the hilly green terrains of Panvel, the property offers exclusive 1, 2 and 2.5 BHK residences along with lifestyle amenities. Its beautiful interiors, open landscaping, and surrounding greens will make your life here nothing short of paradise! Along with offerings of a lively countryside, Panvel also bestows a developed city by your home. It is a happening vicinity that offers the finest landmarks, schools, and healthcare - Delhi Public School, Orion Mall, Lifeline Hospital, and Amity University, all within 20 minutes of the property. The upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport is merely 15 minutes away, thus offering an elite neighbourhood in the future! Furthermore - the Panvel Railway Station, Thane Belapur Road, and Sion Panvel Expressway also ensure that you can quickly reach suburbs such as Belapur, Vashi, Airoli, Thane, Sion, Kurla, and Chembur. In short - Marathon Nexzone is where you can enjoy a lifestyle near majestic mountains without having to sacrifice city life.

About Marathon Realty

Founded in 1969, Marathon Realty develops residential towers and commercial spaces in locales such as Dombivli, Lower Parel, Panvel, Byculla, Kalyan-Shil Road, and Mulund. Over the course of 50 years, Marathon Realty has provided lavish homes for over 10,000 families. The firm further has more than 15,000 residences under development.

We are a 54+ year old, Mumbai based real estate development company that has completed over 100 projects in the city. We are currently building several townships in the fastest growing neighborhoods, affordable housing projects, ultra-luxury skyscrapers, small offices and large business centers. Our projects are spread across the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR)
